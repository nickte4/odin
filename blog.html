Local Dog Rescue.

Authored by: Nick Topacio

The Situation

According to locals, a tiny Shih-Tzu puppy was found trapped high up in the branches of a massive oak tree. A shopkeeper nearby called the local fire department for help.

The Help

With the help of both locals and firemen, the puppy safely made it to the ground where his owner, a 6 year-old, quickly comforted and embraced the shaken pup.
